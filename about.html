About Us

Ngaali Technologies Ltd.

We make it Happen.

Ngaali Technologies Ltd was incorporated in 2024 as a Limited Liability Company Registered in Uganda under the 2012 Company Act by the Uganda Registration services Bureau (URSB).

We are a holding company with a special business niche in solar energy, solar powered systems, HVAC&R system, ElectroMechanial systems installation,and maintenance. Our skilled and specialist professionals offer unmatched services to our growing client base across our specialist fields of agri-business, infrastructure, logistics, industry and Factories.

Our Purpose

We strive to turn visions into reality and build vibrant communities, focusing on integrity, quality, and innovation to create meaningful structures that enhance lives.

Our Future

We envision a future where innovation meets sustainability. As we look ahead, our commitment to excellence will remain steadfast, guiding our projects and shaping the communities we serve.

Our Principles

Happy Customers: Your satisfaction is our priority. Integrity: We value honesty and transparency at work. Quality: We prioritize excellence in materials and craftsmanship.

Our Story

We make it Happen.

Our story is one of evolution, innovation, and a steadfast commitment to creating a sustainable future. Founded in 2024, we began as a small construction firm driven by a passion for quality and community. Over the year, we have expanded our services to include solar energy solutions, CCTV camera installation, among other services positioning ourselves as a multifaceted company dedicated to enhancing both infrastructure and safety in the communities we serve.

But our story isn't over yet! We're constantly looking for ways to improve and expand our services, always striving to ensure customer satisfaction. Join us on our journey to build a sustainable and secure future!
